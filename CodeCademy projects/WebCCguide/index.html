Layout Theory

So here we go again. Another site going up. Hopefully you'll read this part this time.

Grab the eyes on the header. Use a background fitting the mood. Links on the top, don't let them get guady. Keep it clean neat and spaced out. No need to feel like clowns in a circus. Nice neat and organized, to the point with a thought out plan.
